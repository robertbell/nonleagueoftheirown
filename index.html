Frequently asked questions

Why are you doing this?

The European Super League proposal really hit a nerve. Let's refocus on the real heart of football: grass roots, non-league.

Will clubs get 100% of the ticket price?

Absolutely! 100% of the ticket money will go to the clubs. We'll include an option to 'tip' the club staff as well, should you wish to!

How will the community work?

Currently thinking everyone will signup to go to the same game. It's like one big away day, somewhere in the U.K. Feedback more than welcome on this and all aspects!
